# Problem 09

Write a C program to multiply two matrices.


## Examples
<b>Input<b><br>
<pre>
Enter elements in matrix A of size 3x3:
1  2  3
4  5  6
7  8  9

Enter elements in matrix B of size 3x3:
1  2  3
4  5  6
7  8  9</pre>
<b>Output<b><br>
<pre>
Product of matrix A * B =
30  36  42
66  81  96
102 126 150</pre>
